The Grindset Blueprint

The Instructor Grindset is a lifestyle supplement for young snowsports instructors who want to build a work hard, play hard career. We will work with you to build a year-round career development strategy so you can turn seasonal ski school work into a relatively lucrative and worthwhile career.

Understand your environment

By understanding the state of the industry and identifying your needs and interests, we get to focus on the things that really matter to you. When building your plan, we will consider your priorities and goals. Your plan will most likely include training for:

  • Education
  • Fitness
  • Creative willpower
  • On-snow enjoyment and progression

Tracking your progress

We will cover how to assess your progress across all training modalities.

The Instructor Grindset provides off and in-season programming.
