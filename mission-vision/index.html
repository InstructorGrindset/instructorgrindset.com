Mission & Vision

Do you ever teach an awe inspiring lesson only to feel off on the next one? The instructor grindset helps snowsports instructors solve this painful problem in their own way, no matter where they are.

Mission

Helping ski instructors become athletes & irresistible coaches with a season plan and lesson log.

Vision

A community of snowsports instructors who move like athletes, and grow the sport by teaching to the guests' broadest definitions of fun.
