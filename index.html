My Story

For the last 16 ski seasons, I have traveled and worked in 10 different ski resorts in hopes of understanding how some ski schools are designed to enchant children in the mountains. I am willing to talk to anyone who might want to know more about my experience. If you have any questions, contact me at the bottom of this page or interact with me via YouTube comments.

Mission

Helping ski instructors become athletes & irresistible coaches.

Vision

A community of snowsports instructors who move like athletes, and grow the sport by teaching to the guests' broadest definitions of fun.
