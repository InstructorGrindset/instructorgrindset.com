How to use your exported plan
  1. Open a blank Google Sheet and paste with Cmd+V or Ctrl+V.
  2. The calendar fills automatically — weeks as rows, days as columns.
  3. Each cell contains the activity tags you scheduled in the builder.
  4. Replace placeholder text with your specific exercise, book, or activity choice.
