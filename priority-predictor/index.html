Priority List Predictor

Most ski schools assign lessons based on a priority list. Your position on that list determines whether you get the advanced private lesson or get sent home. This tool estimates where you would land based on the factors that actually drive priority: employment status, certification level, additional credentials, and years of experience. It uses 2022 PSIA-AASI national membership data for certification scoring.
